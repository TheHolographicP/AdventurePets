About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Adventure Pets, where we believe that every pet deserves a life filled with excitement, exploration, and endless adventures. As an animal rescue organization, we are dedicated to connecting adventurous souls with their perfect companions, ensuring that both humans and animals can embark on thrilling journeys together. At Adventure Pets, we specialize in finding furry friends who are ready to hit the trails, scale mountains, and explore the great outdoors alongside their devoted humans. Our mission is to match adventurous spirits with pets who share their zest for life, creating bonds that last a lifetime. Whether you're seeking a loyal hiking buddy, a fearless trailblazer, or a spirited companion for your outdoor escapades, Adventure Pets is here to make your dreams of exploration a reality. Join us on the journey to find your ultimate adventure companion today!

Our History

Since its inception in 1998, Adventure Pets has been on a remarkable journey dedicated to rescuing and connecting pets with adventurous souls. Over the years, our organization has celebrated numerous milestones and significant events that have shaped our mission and impact. From humble beginnings as a local rescue group to becoming a leading force in promoting outdoor companionship, Adventure Pets has continuously expanded its reach and influence. We've organized countless adoption events, partnered with outdoor enthusiasts and brands, and facilitated life-changing matches between pets and their adventurous humans. Each year, we've witnessed heartwarming success stories of pets finding their forever homes and embarking on thrilling adventures with their new families. Through dedication, passion, and unwavering commitment, Adventure Pets has become synonymous with the spirit of exploration and the joy of outdoor companionship. As we look back on our journey, we remain inspired by the countless lives we've touched and the bonds we've forged between pets and their adventurous companions. Here's to many more years of rescuing, exploring, and creating unforgettable adventures together.
